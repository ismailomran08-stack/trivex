What We Do

Healthcare-Compliant Construction

Medical and dental construction demands a higher standard. From infection control during active clinic renovations to medical-grade plumbing systems and enhanced HVAC requirements, every detail matters. We build healthcare facilities that meet the strictest regulatory standards — because patient safety and your professional reputation depend on it. Our team coordinates with your equipment suppliers, regulatory bodies, and design consultants to deliver a facility that is ready for patients on day one.

Medical-Grade Plumbing Systems

Specialized plumbing for medical gas, vacuum systems, compressed air, sterilization equipment, and dental chair connections.

Enhanced HVAC & Ventilation

Positive/negative pressure rooms, HEPA filtration, fresh air requirements, and temperature-controlled environments for patient comfort and safety.

Sanitation-Compliant Finishes

Non-porous surfaces, seamless flooring, antimicrobial materials, and easy-to-clean wall finishes that meet healthcare hygiene standards.

Exam Room & Operatory Construction

Purpose-built examination rooms, dental operatories, and treatment suites with proper lighting, utilities, and cabinetry.

Surgical Suite Build-Outs

Sterile environments for minor surgical procedures with proper ventilation, lighting, scrub stations, and equipment infrastructure.

X-Ray Room Shielding

Lead-lined walls, floors, and ceilings for dental and medical X-ray rooms that meet radiation safety regulations.

Accessibility Compliance

AODA and Ontario Building Code accessibility standards including wheelchair access, barrier-free washrooms, and accessible exam rooms.

Equipment Infrastructure Planning

We plan electrical, plumbing, and structural support for your specific medical equipment before walls go up — preventing costly retrofits.

FAQ

Medical Construction Questions

Common questions from healthcare professionals and clinic owners.

Do you understand healthcare building codes?
Yes. We build medical and dental clinics to meet Ontario Building Code healthcare requirements, including enhanced ventilation, medical gas systems, infection control barriers, sanitation-compliant surfaces, and accessibility standards. Every build is designed to pass inspection on the first try.
Can you coordinate with medical equipment suppliers?
Absolutely. We work directly with your equipment suppliers to ensure proper infrastructure is in place before equipment arrives — electrical capacity, plumbing connections, compressed air lines, vacuum systems, and structural support for heavy imaging equipment.
How do you handle infection control during construction?
For renovations of active clinics, we implement full infection control risk assessments (ICRA) including negative pressure barriers, HEPA filtration, sealed construction zones, and dedicated entry/exit paths for workers. Patient safety is never compromised during the build process.
Do you build dental operatories?
Yes. We build complete dental operatories including plumbing for dental chairs, compressed air and vacuum systems, nitrous oxide lines, X-ray infrastructure, and cabinetry. We understand the unique mechanical requirements of dental offices and coordinate with your equipment dealer for seamless installation.
