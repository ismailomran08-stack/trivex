FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Trivex.

What types of construction projects does Trivex specialize in?
Commercial construction - specifically franchise fit-outs, restaurant builds, medical and dental clinics, and office/retail build-outs across all Canadian provinces.
Do you handle multi-location franchise rollouts?
Yes - franchise construction is our core business. We've completed 250+ builds with repeatable execution and brand compliance across 8 provinces.
What regions do you operate in?
We operate across Ontario, Atlantic Canada (NS, NB, NF, PEI), Manitoba, Saskatchewan, and Alberta. We manage logistics across provincial lines without sacrificing quality.
How is Trivex different from other contractors?
We're partner-operated. Every project is managed by an owner - not layered management. You work directly with decision-makers who are personally accountable for your build.
Do you handle permits, inspections, and consultants?
Yes - we coordinate permits, inspections, engineers, designers, and all consultants. You get one point of contact for the entire process.
How fast can you deliver a project?
It depends on scope, but our fastest turnaround was a complete M&M Food Market facelift in 5 days. Most franchise fit-outs are delivered in 6-10 weeks. We'll give you a realistic timeline upfront.
